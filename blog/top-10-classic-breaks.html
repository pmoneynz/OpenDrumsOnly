Top 10 classic breaks

Updated: 2026-02-19

This list focuses on foundational drum breaks that repeatedly shaped hip-hop, jungle, breakbeat, and modern sample-based production. Every selection below links directly to a verifiable OpenDrumsOnly entry.

Method note: this ranking is editorial and based on long-term sample-culture consensus, not a single quantitative dataset. Every item below is verified to exist as a live OpenDrumsOnly entry and links directly to that page.

  1. Amen Brother - Winstons, The: The most recycled break in jungle and drum & bass history. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  2. Funky Drummer - James Brown: The definitive funk break and a hip-hop production cornerstone. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  3. Impeach the President - Honey Drippers, The: Crisp snare and kick combo sampled across eras. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  4. Apache - Incredible Bongo Band: Core early hip-hop and breakdance DNA. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  5. Think (About It) - Lyn Collins: Source of one of the most recognizable vocal/drum fragments in dance music. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  6. Synthetic Substitution - Melvin Bliss: A standard break for boom-bap and golden-era chopping. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  7. Ashley's Roachclip - Soul Searchers, The: Tight, dry groove favored for looping and layering. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  8. It's A New Day, I Turn My Back On Love - Skull Snaps: Heavy, dusty pocket sampled heavily in rap and beats. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  9. The Champ - Mohawks, The: Essential old-school breakbeat with durable punch. OpenDrumsOnly entry
  10. Dance to the Drummer's Beat - Herman Kelly & Life: A staple percussion break in electro and hip-hop circles. OpenDrumsOnly entry

FAQ

What does “classic break” mean here?

It means a drum passage with persistent historical use across genres, not just a personal favorite or one-off cult pick.

Is this a definitive list?

No. It is a concise, high-confidence starting point. Use linked entries to branch into deeper digging by year, style, and artist.

How can I suggest a missing classic?

Use the submission page and include as much source detail as possible so we can verify and add it efficiently.
